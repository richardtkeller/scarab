Platform Overview

The Smart Sub-Q Dot platform merges next-generation biosensing with ultra-low-power electronics and safe, secure clinical data integration. Unlike wearables that rely on surface-level signals, the Sub-Q Dot captures internal biomarkers with deep clinical relevance — providing earlier, more reliable indicators of deterioration.

The platform is designed around four pillars:

  • Miniaturised implantable sensor
  • Multi-year energy autonomy
  • Encrypted wireless communication
  • Clinician-facing analytics and alerting

Core Components

1. Micro-Scale Biosensor

Captures high-value physiological signals such as oxygenation, glucose, lactate, pH, inflammation markers, or strain — depending on clinical configuration.

2. Ultra-Low-Power Electronics

Custom ASIC architecture ensures multi-year operation with minimal energy draw, enabling autonomous sensing with no charging required.

3. Secure Wireless Telemetry

Encrypted BLE or RF communication securely sends processed data to a smartphone or receiver, then to the cloud environment.

4. Cloud Analytics Engine

Clinicians receive pattern-based alerts and longitudinal insight streams with minimal false positives, powered by medically tuned analytics models.

Full System Architecture

The Smart Sub-Q Dot system is built for seamless integration across patient home use, clinician workflows, and digital health infrastructure.

  • Implant measures physiological signals continuously.
  • Phone or wearable receiver collects data automatically.
  • Secure cloud pipelines process and score signals.
  • Clinicians receive dashboards, alerts, and trend analysis.
