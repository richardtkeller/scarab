About the Sensor

The Smart Sub-Q Dot is a tiny, rice-grain-sized implant designed to measure 2–3 high-value physiological signals continuously. It can be configured for cardiopulmonary, metabolic, or ortho/post-op use and transmits securely to a patient phone and clinician dashboard.

Why this approach?

  • Low patient burden: no charging, no daily interaction.
  • Clinical value: measures signals non-capturable by wearables (local tissue oxygenation, intracardiac trends, local biomarkers).
  • Workflow friendly: integrates into clinician dashboards and EHR via secure APIs.

Typical configurations

Cardiopulmonary
StO₂ (local tissue oxygenation), local temperature, HR/HRV trends — early detection of HF decompensation and respiratory infections.
Metabolic
Continuous glucose ± lactate and pH — early metabolic instability and sepsis risk signalling.
Ortho / Post-Op
Local temperature, pH, mechanical strain — early prosthetic infection and healing assessment.

Scarab Medical combines miniaturised sensing, robust in-vivo calibration, and lightweight on-device signal processing to deliver clinically meaningful metrics with minimal patient burden.
