About Us — Scarab Medical

Scarab Medical develops clinical-grade implantable sensors addressing major unmet needs such as early heart failure detection, metabolic instability, infection surveillance, and orthopaedic implant monitoring. Our work is grounded in validated sensing, robust in-vivo calibration and clinician-aligned analytics.

About the Sensor

The Smart Sub-Q Dot is a rice-grain-sized fully implantable micro-sensor designed to deliver continuous, actionable physiological insight with zero maintenance burden.

  • Office-based 5–10 minute implantation
  • No charging, no patches, no daily tasks
  • Encrypted transmission to patient app + clinician dashboard
  • Configurations: cardiopulmonary, metabolic, ortho/post-op

The Technology

The platform combines miniaturised biosensing, multi-year energy strategies, secure wireless communication and cloud-based medical analytics. Designed to reduce alert fatigue and improve early detection of deterioration.

  • Validated sensing against clinical gold standards
  • Energy harvesting or micro-battery autonomy
  • Encrypted implant → phone → cloud pipeline
  • Clinician-tuned analytics for trustworthy alerts
