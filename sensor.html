What is the Smart Sub-Q Dot?

The Smart Sub-Q Dot is a fully implantable, rice-grain–sized microsensor designed for continuous, high-value physiological monitoring. Unlike wearables, it captures internal biomarkers that provide much earlier warning of clinical deterioration.

Implanted in a simple 5–10 minute office procedure, it requires zero patient maintenance: no charging, no dressings, and no calibration. It integrates seamlessly with clinician dashboards and EHR systems.

Key Advantages

  • Implant-and-forget simplicity
  • Multi-year lifespan
  • Captures non-invasive–invisible biomarkers
  • Automatic secure data transmission
  • No lifestyle restrictions; MRI-conditional

Available Sensor Configurations

The Smart Sub-Q Dot is available in targeted clinical variants, each optimized for specific early-warning indicators.

Cardiopulmonary Version

StO₂ (tissue oxygenation), local temperature, HR/HRV — detects early heart failure decompensation and respiratory infections.

Metabolic Version

Continuous glucose, lactate, pH — identifies metabolic instability, sepsis risk, and perioperative deterioration.

Orthopedic & Post-Op Version

Temperature, pH, mechanical strain — early detection of prosthetic joint infection and healing complications.

How the System Works

The Smart Sub-Q Dot is designed for effortless clinical adoption.

  • Sensor continuously measures selected physiological signals.
  • Data flows to the patient’s smartphone or low-profile receiver.
  • Encrypted transmission sends data to a secure cloud.
  • Clinicians receive processed trends, alerts, and longitudinal insights.
  • No patient interaction is required unless an alert is generated.
